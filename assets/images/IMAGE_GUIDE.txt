NOTES:
------
- Images will gracefully fallback if missing (show placeholder or hide)
- JPG or PNG both work
- Recommended sizes:
  - Room: whatever resolution looks good (will scale)
  - Portrait images: 150x150 to 300x300 (square works best)
  - Item photos: 400-600px wide
  - Top 8: 60x60 to 100x100

PORTRAIT MOOD MAPPING:
----------------------
The dialog system uses different portraits based on conversational mood:
- smiling1/smiling2: Warm, welcoming moments
- thoughtful1/thoughtful2: Reflective, explaining complex ideas
- funny1/funny2: Jokes, self-deprecating humor
- excited: Talking about passion projects, music, things you love
- serious: Professional approach, methodology explanations
- default: Fallback for any missing portraits
