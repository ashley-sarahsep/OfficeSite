Mobile Experience Survey Guide

Quick & Effective Feedback Collection for Pop-Up and Touring Activations

Collecting feedback directly from mobile experience visitors provides invaluable insights into the attendee experience. This guide outlines a streamlined approach to gathering meaningful data through a brief, engaging survey that visitors can complete on-site or shortly after their experience.

The recommended survey is designed to be completed in under 2 minutes while capturing key metrics and sentiment that will help optimize future mobile activations.

Quick

7 questions max to respect time and maximize completions

Actionable

Data that directly informs program improvements

Engaging

Fun and on-brand, not a corporate questionnaire

Core Principles for Effective Attendee Surveys

Ultra-Brief Format

Keep to 7 questions maximum. Visitors are there for the experience, not paperwork. Brevity increases completion rates and goodwill.

Mobile-First Design

Large tap targets, minimal typing, responsive design that works even with spotty event Wi-Fi.

Prioritize Closed Questions

Multiple-choice, rating scales, and single-selection make completion faster and analysis more straightforward.

One Open-Ended Option

Include just one well-crafted text question to capture unexpected insights and let visitors share what matters most.

Fun, On-Brand Tone

Maintain your brand's spirit with conversational tone, branded visuals, and creative rating scales.

Clear Value Exchange

Offer a small incentive (digital coupon, exclusive content) to show you value their feedback and time.

Multiple Collection Methods

QR codes, text-to-survey, email follow-up, and tablets on-site for immediate completion.

Consistent Core Questions

Maintain standard questions across all events for longitudinal data while allowing 1-2 event-specific additions.

Core Experience Questions

Rating Scale
How would you rate your overall experience today?
  • Absolutely amazing!
  • Really good
  • Good / Okay / Disappointing
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Overall satisfaction benchmark, event-to-event comparison
Single Selection
What was your favorite part of the visit?
  • Trying products / Unique setup
  • Talking with staff / Photos
  • Learning something new / Other
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Experience element prioritization, key attraction factors
Yes/No + Selection
Did anything about your visit surprise or delight you?
  • No, it was what I expected
  • Yes, the [specific elements]
  • Yes, something else (specify)
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Expectation vs. reality gap, unexpected delight factors
Multiple Selection
Which activities did you participate in today? (Select all)
  • Product sampling / Photos
  • Staff conversation / Social sharing
  • Learned about brand
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Engagement depth measurement, activity prioritization

Brand Connection

Scale Question
After today, how likely are you to purchase our products in the next month?
  • Definitely will (5)
  • Very likely to Definitely won't (4-1)
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Purchase intent impact, conversion potential
Yes/No with Follow-up
Did today's visit change how you feel about our brand?
  • Yes, more positive now
  • No change, already positive
  • No change, neutral/unchanged
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Brand sentiment impact, existing vs. new customer effect
NPS Style
How likely are you to recommend visiting us to a friend?
  • 0-10 scale with labels
  • Optional follow-up: Why?
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Net promoter score, word-of-mouth potential
Multiple Selection
Which words best describe our brand after today? (Select up to 3)
  • Fun / Innovative / Premium
  • Friendly / Authentic / Creative
INSIGHTS ENABLED: Brand attribute association, messaging alignment

Implementation Best Practices

Collection Timing

  • Offer survey immediately after interaction
  • Position near exit, not blocking flow
  • Send follow-ups within 24 hours
  • Test different times for responsiveness

Multiple Methods

  • QR codes on signage and giveaways
  • Text-to-survey option
  • Tablets for on-site completion
  • Email for later completion
  • Paper backup for connectivity issues

Incentive Strategies

  • Digital coupons or discount codes
  • Exclusive digital content
  • Entry into prize drawings
  • Small branded items on-site
  • Highlight feedback impact

Staff Training

  • Script 10-second conversational invite
  • Train to mention survey naturally
  • Friendly team competition for completions
  • Explain how feedback is used
  • Thank visitors who complete

Technical Setup

  • Offline capability with sync
  • Optimized for all devices
  • Quick loading on slow connections
  • Readable in bright sunlight
  • Clear progress indicators

Rotation Strategy

  • 4-5 core questions that never change
  • Rotate 1-2 questions monthly
  • Event-specific questions as needed
  • A/B test new questions
  • Refresh set quarterly

Measuring What Matters

These carefully crafted questions balance brevity with insight, capturing meaningful visitor feedback without disrupting the experience. By implementing this streamlined approach, you'll gain direct insight into visitor perceptions, preferences, and experiences that refine your program while strengthening connections with your audience.

The best survey is one visitors will actually complete. Keep it brief, mobile-friendly, and offer a small incentive to maximize response rates while collecting the data that matters most.
